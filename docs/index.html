Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is tiling and why should I care?

Tiling automatically arranges your windows to fill your screen without overlapâ€”like tiles on a floor. Instead of manually resizing and positioning windows, FancyWM organizes them automatically as you open them. This means more screen space used efficiently and less time wasted on window management.

Will this slow down my computer?

No. FancyWM uses less than 1% CPU because it's lightweight and efficient. Many users report improved performance since they spend less time juggling windows and can focus on their actual work.

Do I need to learn complicated keyboard shortcuts?

Not at all! FancyWM works perfectly with your mouse. Drag windows to rearrange them. Or use keyboard shortcuts if you prefer. The main hotkey is Shift+Win, and from there you can use arrow keys, H for horizontal, V for vertical, and S for stacked layouts. No memorization required.

Will it work with games and other applications?

Most applications work great with FancyWM. For applications that don't play well with tiling, you can easily toggle floating mode with one keystroke (Shift+Win+F) to use traditional window management.

What if I don't like it?

No commitment needed! You can disable tiling with one keystroke (Shift+Win+F11) or uninstall FancyWM like any Windows application. It's completely reversible and zero-risk to try.

Is it stable enough for daily use?

Yes! FancyWM is available on the Microsoft Store, actively maintained, and used by thousands of developers daily. It's production-ready and reliable.
